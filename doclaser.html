Laser Cutting Documentation

This intensive module introduces learners to laser cutting technology, covering essential concepts, hardware and software, file preparation, material selection, safe operation, troubleshooting, and practical projects to master digital fabrication skills.

laser_cutting Day 1 – Intro & Applications

  • What is laser cutting?
  • Types of lasers (CO2, Fiber, Diode)
  • Common uses (wood, acrylic, etc.)
  • Safety basics
  • ✅ Activity: List 5 laser-cut items.

hardware Day 2 – Hardware & Software

  • Laser cutter parts (laser tube, bed, exhaust, etc.)
  • Software: LaserCad (version)
  • Vector vs Raster, file types (.svg, .dxf)
  • ✅ Activity: design a basic shape

design_services Day 3 – Design Basics

  • Cut vs engrave setup
  • Design tips for press fit
  • ✅ Activity: small box design and engraving

layers Day 4 – Materials & Settings

  • Materials: wood, acrylic (types), paper, cardboard, etc.
  • Power/speed settings
  • Testing cuts
  • ✅ Activity: run a speed/power test grid on cardboard

settings Day 5 – Operating the Laser

  • Focusing, bed setup, air assist, exhaust
  • Sending files, running job
  • Safety checks (emergency stop)
  • ✅ Activity: cut your first real design

build Day 6 – Project Day

  • Make something useful (stand, box, 3D)
  • File prep, nesting, assembly
  • ✅ Activity: complete & document your project

fact_check Day 7 – Review & Maintenance

  • Common issues (burn, incomplete cut)
  • Basic maintenance (cleaning, lens, mirrors)
  • Project showcase
  • ✅ Activity: present final work & reflect
